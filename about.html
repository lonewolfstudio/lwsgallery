Graphic Designer

Educational Background

Originally, I was a Illustrator and Graphic Designer, executing catalog print layouts and similar projects. As a student, I trained as a painting and drawing major. I completed my Bachelors Degree at the School of the Art Institute of Chicago in 2001.

Print Production and Design - 1984 to 1989

Intern and Assistant positions in various advertising agencies that produced "traditional" print projects were followed by a year of digital production training through the "MacUniversity" training group and others. This was followed by several years of digital production traditional illustration projects as a contractor. Clients included retail catalog agencies and marketing agencies, where digital production techniques were being introduced.

Illustration

I was successful in developing several clients that required product illustration and offered opportunities to introduce digital production techniques into their worflows. Revell-Monogram plastic hobby kits, Sportmart (merged with the Sports Authority), Lakeland Boating Magazine, Motorola and several printing companies were served by illustrations and digital producion workflows.

Architectural illustration of a retail and restaurant concept called Archer's Dream.

Illustration

Traditional product illustrations, caricatures, editorial illustrations for magazines. Digital illustrations were employed to streamline the workflows.
