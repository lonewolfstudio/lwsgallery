Methode.com Relaunch | Corporate Web Site Aims for Web 2.0 Upgrade

 Web Site Design

Fourteen divisions of Methode Electrtonics needed a new look and feel in anticipation of thegrowth of eCommerce in the era of "Web 2.0".

Web-site Steering Committee

First steps in the process included inclusion in a Steering Commitee, and creation of a mission statement. Selection of team leads and the setting of mission parameters were a "concensus" of the executive staff of the commitee.

Mission Statement

"Seek the most efficient upgrade path to enabling Methode.com to be a sales tool for online product information, sales promotion and product advertising materials". A web-store application was also proposed as a separate case study, and later dropped.
