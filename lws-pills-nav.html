MacArthurPTA Web Site

MacArthur PTA Home Page - Site Admin and Content Manager - Jan 2013

Complete redesign for the PHP-based PTA site at MacArthur Elementary School in Hoffman Estates Illinois was a side-project for the academic years 2010 though 2013.

Mission and Goals
  • Update content for main content pages
  • Administrate User Accounts and access
  • Development for a planned move to a mobile-capable or mobile-responsive site
  • Choose a distributed framework for active-media content such as Wordpress or Drupal
Audience and User Profiles
  • Not-for-Profit organization with self-governing stakeholder group with School District oversight and shared resources
  • Two nominally equal User Groups; Parents and Teachers
  • The Parents are usually the most active and are elected on an annual or bi-annual basis
  • Content is driven by scheduling for school-related events, fund-raising efforts and student activities
  • Initially required weekly and monthly updates with only minimum automation capabilities
Mobile-Responsive User Interface Design

The Design of the mobile-responsive site started with the UI design for mobile phone devices, because that was the greatest need. The content could easily be refactored into a Wordpress database, or more likely, a series of jQuery calls to secure lists.
